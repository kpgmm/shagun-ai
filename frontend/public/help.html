📖 Application Overview

Shagun AI is a digital platform designed for Indian families and event organizers to manage shagun — the traditional gift of money given at weddings, naamkarans, griha pravesh ceremonies, and pujas. Instead of manually recording cash and UPI payments in notebooks, Shagun AI gives you a real-time digital ledger with guest management, WhatsApp notifications, and PDF reports — all in one place.

🎉
Event Management
Create and manage multiple events. Activate them on the day and mark them complete when done.
📝
Live Entry Feed
Record Cash, UPI, and Gift entries in real time. Auto-matches guests by phone number.
👥
Guest List
Maintain a guest list with RSVP tracking. Import guests from previous events in one click.
📅
Activities
Break your event into sub-events (Mehndi, Garba, Reception) and track entries per activity.
💬
WhatsApp Invites
Send personalised WhatsApp invitations and payment confirmations via Twilio.
📊
PDF Reports
Download a professionally formatted PDF summary for the whole event or per activity.

Who is this for?

  • Event hosts — families organising weddings, naming ceremonies, or religious events
  • Operators — a trusted person at the entrance recording incoming guests and payments
  • Family administrators — anyone who wants a clean, searchable record of shagun received
🚀 Getting Started

Creating your account

  1. Register
    Go to the app and click Register. Enter your name, a 10-digit mobile number, and a password.
  2. Log in
    Use your mobile number and password to sign in. You will be taken to My Events automatically.
  3. Create your first event
    Click New Event, fill in the event details, then add at least one activity to proceed.
ℹ️
First-time users: Your event starts in Draft status. You must click Activate Event on the dashboard before you can receive Razorpay UPI payments linked to that event.
🎉 Events

An Event is the top-level container — a wedding, naamkaran, or puja. Everything (guests, entries, activities, reports) belongs to one event.

Creating an Event

  1. Click "New Event"
    Found on the My Events page, top-right.
  2. Fill Event Details (Step 1)
    Enter event name, type, date, host name, village/city, UPI ID, and WhatsApp number.
  3. Add Activities (Step 2)
    Add at least one activity (e.g., Reception). You can add more later. Click Create Event →.
  4. Import Guests (Optional)
    After creation, a dialog offers to import guests from a previous event. Select guests or click Skip.

Event Statuses

💡
Tip: Click Mark Completed when the event ends. The app will redirect you to the Report page so you can download the final PDF immediately.
📅 Activities

Activities are sub-events within an event — e.g., Mehndi, Baraat, Garba, Reception. Every event must have at least one activity. You can track entries, guests, and generate a separate PDF per activity.

Activity Types

Choose from preset types — Mehndi, Sangeet, Garba, Baraat, Wedding, Reception, Naamkaran, Griha Pravesh, Puja — or select Custom and type your own name.

Activity Statuses

StatusMeaning
UpcomingNot yet started
ActiveCurrently running — Razorpay webhook auto-assigns entries here when only one activity is active
CompletedFinished; entries are locked to this activity

Assigning Guests to an Activity

Open an activity → go to the Guests tab → click Manage Guests. Select which guests are attending this specific sub-event. This is informational only — it does not restrict who can make a payment.

⚠️
Note: You cannot delete an activity that has entries linked to it. Delete or reassign those entries first.
📝 Recording Entries

An entry records a single payment or gift from one guest or party. Navigate to Event Dashboard → Record Entry, or open an activity and use the entry form there.

Payment Modes

ModeRequired FieldsOptional Fields
💵 Cash Name, Village, Amount Phone, Notes
📱 UPI Name, Village, Amount Phone, UTR Number, Notes
🎁 Gift Name, Village, Gift Item (description) Estimated Value (₹), Notes
ℹ️
Gift entries: The amount is optional for gifts. If you enter an estimated value (e.g., ₹500 for a gold ring), it will be included in the total collection. The gift item description (e.g., "Gold ring", "Saree") appears in the Amount column of the live feed.

Guest Autocomplete

When you start typing a name in the Guest Name field, the app searches your guest list and shows matches. Clicking a suggestion fills in the name, village, and phone automatically.

Amount Presets

For Cash and UPI modes, quick-select common shagun amounts — ₹101, ₹201, ₹501, ₹1,001, ₹1,100, ₹2,100, ₹5,001.

Razorpay UPI (Automatic)

When a guest scans the event's Razorpay QR code and pays, the entry is created automatically — no manual input needed. The system also tries to match the phone number to a guest in your list.

Editing and Deleting Entries

In the Live Entry Feed, each row has edit (✏️) and delete (🗑) icons. Deleting requires a reason (e.g., "Duplicate entry"). Deleted entries are archived, not permanently removed.

👥 Guest Management

Navigate to Event Dashboard → Guests to manage your guest list. The guest list is used for autocomplete in the entry form, RSVP tracking, and WhatsApp invitations.

Adding Guests

  • Add Guest — Add one guest at a time with name, phone, village, and relation side.
  • Bulk Upload — Upload an Excel (.xlsx) or CSV file. The app lets you map your file's columns to the required fields before importing.
  • Import from Event — Copy the guest list from one of your previous events. Select which guests to import (all are pre-selected). Duplicate phone numbers are automatically skipped.

RSVP Tracking

The top of the Guests page shows a summary card: Total Invited / Coming / Not Coming / No Response. Guests reply to the WhatsApp invite with HAAN (coming) or NA (not coming), and the app updates their RSVP automatically.

Filtering

Use the All Relations and All RSVP dropdowns to filter the guest table. Filters can be combined.

💬 WhatsApp Invitations

Shagun AI sends personalised WhatsApp messages via Twilio. Three types of messages are sent:

Message TypeTriggerContent
InviteYou click Send InviteEvent name, date, village, RSVP instructions (reply HAAN / NA)
Payment ConfirmationEntry created with a phone numberConfirms the amount received
RSVP AcknowledgmentGuest replies HAAN or NAThank-you message

Sending Invitations

Three ways to send invites from the Guests page:

  • Send Invites (top button) — Sends to all guests who have not yet received an invite.
  • Per-row Send icon (✈) — Sends to one specific guest. A blue icon means invite was already sent (re-send allowed).
  • Selection-based — Tick the checkboxes on individual rows, then click Send Invites (N) in the action bar that appears above the table.

⚠️
Twilio requirement: WhatsApp messaging requires a configured Twilio account. Contact your administrator if invitations are not being sent.
📊 Reports

Navigate to Event Dashboard → Report. Two PDF reports are available:

Event Report (Full PDF)

  • Event details (date, host, village, status)
  • Financial summary — Total Collected, UPI breakdown, Cash breakdown, Gift count and value
  • Guest RSVP summary (if guests were invited)
  • Complete ledger — all entries in a table with name, village, amount, mode, and activity

Activity Report (Per-Activity PDF)

Open an Activity → Report tab → Download Activity Report (PDF). This is scoped to only the entries for that activity.

💡
Gift entries in reports: Gift rows show the item description (e.g., "Saree") in the Amount column. If an estimated value was entered, it is included in the Total Collected figure.
🔄 Example Workflows
🎊 Workflow 1: Setting up a Wedding Event
  1. Create the event
    New Event → fill details → add activities (Mehndi, Baraat, Reception) → click Create Event.
  2. Import or add guests
    Use "Import from Event" if guests are same as last year, or upload an Excel file with the new list.
  3. Send invitations
    Go to Guests page → Send Invites (uninvited only). Guests receive a WhatsApp message with date, venue, and RSVP link.
  4. Activate on the event day
    Dashboard → Activate Event. The Razorpay QR is now live for digital payments.
  5. Record entries throughout the day
    Operator opens Record Entry. Type the guest name — autocomplete fills village/phone. Select Cash/UPI/Gift and enter amount. Save.
  6. Complete and download report
    Dashboard → Mark Completed → Download PDF Report.
💵 Workflow 2: Recording a Gift Entry
  1. Open Record Entry
    Navigate to your active event → Record Entry.
  2. Type the guest's name
    Start typing — if the guest is in your list, select from the dropdown.
  3. Select 🎁 Gift
    Click the Gift button in the Payment Mode section.
  4. Enter gift details
    Type what was gifted in the Gift Item / Description field (e.g., "Silver coins"). Optionally enter an estimated value.
  5. Save
    Click Save Entry. The entry appears in the live feed with a purple GIFT badge.
📨 Workflow 3: Selection-based Invite Send
  1. Go to Guests page
    Event Dashboard → Guests.
  2. Filter if needed
    Use the Relation or RSVP filter to narrow the list (e.g., "No Response" only).
  3. Select guests
    Click the checkbox in the header to select all visible guests, or tick individual rows.
  4. Send
    Click Send Invites (N) in the action bar. Invites are queued and sent in the background.
✅ Best Practices
  • Add guests before the event day — autocomplete only works if the guest is already in your list. Upload your guest list in advance.
  • Always enter phone numbers — phone numbers enable guest matching for Razorpay UPI payments and automatic WhatsApp confirmations.
  • Activate only one activity at a time — when exactly one activity is Active, Razorpay webhook payments are automatically assigned to it.
  • Use Gift mode for physical items — even if you do not know the value, record the item description (e.g., "Gold necklace") for a complete record.
  • Download the PDF before closing the event — once you mark an event Completed, the Report page shows the final totals. Download it while everything is fresh.
  • Always provide a reason when deleting entries — deletion reasons appear in the audit trail, helping you reconcile records later.
  • Import guests from last year's event — use "Import from Event" to reuse your guest list across family events rather than re-entering names each time.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Entry form shows "Amount must be positive"

This error appears for Cash or UPI entries when the amount is 0 or empty. Enter a positive amount, or switch to Gift mode if this is a physical gift with no monetary value.

Guest autocomplete not showing suggestions

Suggestions appear only after typing at least 2 characters. The guest must already be added to the guest list for this event. Also ensure the dropdown is not already closed — click the name field again after typing.

Live feed not updating in real time

The live feed uses a WebSocket connection (shown by the green dot). If it shows "Connecting…":

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Refresh the page — the connection re-establishes on page load.
  • Entries saved by other operators will appear the next time the page loads if WebSocket is unavailable.

PDF report download does not start

  • Check that your browser is not blocking pop-ups — the PDF opens in a new tab.
  • Ensure the event has at least one entry; an empty report is still generated but shows no data.

Offline banner appears ("No internet connection")

The app detected that your device is offline. Entries entered while offline will not be saved. Wait for the connection to restore (the banner disappears automatically), then re-enter the data.

🚫
Important: Do not close the browser tab while an entry is being submitted. If you see "Saving…", wait for the confirmation before moving away.

Activity deletion blocked ("Activity has entries")

You cannot delete an activity that has payment entries linked to it. Edit those entries to remove the activity link, or delete the entries themselves (with a reason), then retry the activity deletion.

Duplicate phone number error when adding a guest

Each phone number can appear only once per event. If you get this error, the guest is already in your list — use the search/filter to find them. If you're importing from another event, duplicates are automatically skipped.
